# Social Media Accounts

Manage your connected social media accounts in DulyPlan.

## Overview

Social media accounts are the foundation of your DulyPlan experience. Connect multiple accounts from different platforms to manage all your social media presence from one place.

## Connecting Accounts

### Supported Platforms

- Facebook (Pages and Profiles)
- Instagram (Business Accounts)
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn (Personal and Company Pages)
- TikTok
- Threads
- YouTube
- Pinterest
- Telegram
- WhatsApp

### Connection Process

1. Go to **Dashboard** â†’ **Social Accounts**
2. Click **"+ Add Account"**
3. Select your platform
4. Complete OAuth authorization
5. Grant required permissions
6. Account is connected

## Managing Accounts

### Viewing Accounts

- See all connected accounts in the Social Accounts section
- View account status and connection date
- Check account type (Personal, Business, Page)

### Editing Accounts

- Update account display name
- Modify account settings
- Refresh connection tokens

### Disconnecting Accounts

- Remove account connection
- Revoke OAuth permissions
- Clear account data

## Account Status

Accounts can have different statuses:
- **Connected** - Active and ready to use
- **Disconnected** - Needs reconnection
- **Error** - Connection issue, needs attention
