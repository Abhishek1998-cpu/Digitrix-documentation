# Scheduling Posts

Learn how to schedule posts for future publication.

## Scheduling Overview

DulyPlan allows you to schedule posts for any future date and time across multiple timezones.

## How to Schedule

1. Create your post content
2. Click "Schedule" instead of "Publish Now"
3. Select date and time
4. Choose timezone
5. Confirm schedule

## Scheduling Options

- **Specific Date & Time** - Schedule for a precise moment
- **Recurring Posts** - Set up recurring content
- **Bulk Scheduling** - Schedule multiple posts at once

## Timezone Management

- Select from all IANA timezones
- Search timezone by name
- Timezone-aware scheduling
