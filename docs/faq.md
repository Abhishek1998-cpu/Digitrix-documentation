# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about DulyPlan.

## General Questions

### What is DulyPlan?

DulyPlan is a comprehensive social media management platform that helps you plan, schedule, and manage your social media presence across multiple platforms.

### Which platforms does DulyPlan support?

DulyPlan supports Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Threads, YouTube, Pinterest, Telegram, and WhatsApp.

### Is DulyPlan free?

DulyPlan offers a FREE tier with basic features. Additional tiers (TIER1, TIER2, TIER3) are available with more advanced features.

## Account & Authentication

### How do I create an account?

Visit the signup page, enter your email and password, and verify your email address.

### What if I forgot my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the login page to reset your password via email.

### How do I verify my email?

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the verification link.

## Features

### How do I connect my social media accounts?

Go to Dashboard → Social Accounts → "+ Add Account" and follow the OAuth flow for your platform.

### Can I schedule posts in advance?

Yes, you can schedule posts for any future date and time, and choose your preferred timezone.

### How do I create a post?

Navigate to Create, select your platforms and accounts, add content, and publish or schedule.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?

DulyPlan works on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### Is there a mobile app?

Currently, DulyPlan is web-based. Mobile apps may be available in the future.

### How do I report a bug?

Contact support at support@dulyplan.com or use the Error Logs feature in the dashboard.
