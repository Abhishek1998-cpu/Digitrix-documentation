## Key Concepts

### Authentication

Authentication is how DulyPlan verifies you are who you say you are. It involves:
- Creating an account with email and password
- Verifying your email address
- Logging in with your credentials
- Using secure tokens to maintain your session

### Organization Ownership

Every user gets their own organization when they sign up:
- You automatically become the owner
- You have full access to all features
- Your data is isolated in your organization

### Roles (Current System)

DulyPlan uses a simple system:
- **Owner** - You, when you create your organization
- No traditional roles like "Admin" or "User" yet
- Access is based on organization ownership
