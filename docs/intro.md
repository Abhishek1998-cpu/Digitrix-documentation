# Welcome to DulyPlan Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **DulyPlan** - a comprehensive social media management platform that helps you plan, schedule, and manage your social media presence across multiple platforms.

## What is DulyPlan?

DulyPlan is a powerful social media management platform that enables users to:

- **Schedule Posts** - Plan and schedule content across multiple social media platforms
- **Manage Multiple Accounts** - Connect and manage accounts from Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more
- **Content Creation** - Create and edit posts with built-in media editors
- **Analytics** - Track performance and engagement metrics
- **Team Collaboration** - Work together with your team on social media campaigns
- **AI-Powered Features** - Leverage AI for content generation and optimization
